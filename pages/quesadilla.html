Quesadilla Recipe

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Any cheese that melts nicely
  • (Optional) Any meat of your choosing

Instructions

  1. Heat a flat pan to max heat (or lower than that, I'm just impatient)
  2. While the pan is heating up, put your cheese on the tortilla and fold it over as the cover the cheese
  3. Put your torilla on the pan and flip as needed as to not burn either side
  4. (Optional) If you have some cooked meat you can open the tortilla and put the meat in. Fold the tortilla back
  5. Get the quesadilla off the pan once the cheese fully melted and enjoy. (Image below for reference)
