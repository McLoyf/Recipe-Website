Instant Ramen Recipe

Ingredients

  • Instant Ramen Package
  • Water (Preferrably clean)

Cooking Intructions

  1. Get your ramen package
  2. Get a pot
  3. Fill the pot with water
  4. Put the ramen block in the pot along with the flavor packet
  5. Turn on your heating appliance and put it to max heat
  6. Wait a until the ramen looks done and kill the heat
  7. Pour the contents of the pot in a bowl or just eat it straight out the pot. Enjoy it (or don't, it doesn't matter to me)
